VibeFit FAQs: All Your Questions Answered

What is VibeFit and what do we offer? +

VibeFit is a fitness technology brand that combines wearable tech with personalized training programs. We offer devices to track health metrics, optimize workouts, and help users achieve their fitness goals with ease.

Are VibeFit devices waterproof? +

Yes! Most of our devices, including the VibeFit Health Tracker and VibeFit Air Trainers, are water-resistant and designed to handle sweat, rain, and swimming.

How can I sync my VibeFit device with the app? +

Simply download the VibeFit companion app from the App Store or Google Play. Turn on Bluetooth, pair your device, and follow the on-screen instructions to sync your data.
